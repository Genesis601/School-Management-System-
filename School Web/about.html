Our Curriculum

Comprehensive Learning Program

Our curriculum is designed to provide a balanced education that develops the whole child - academically, socially, emotionally, and physically. We follow the Nigerian curriculum while incorporating elements of international best practices.

  • Core subjects: English, Mathematics, Sciences, Social Studies
  • Languages: French and indigenous languages
  • Creative arts: Music, Visual Arts, Drama
  • Physical education and sports
  • Technology and digital literacy
  • Moral instruction and civic education

Our teaching methodology emphasizes inquiry-based learning, collaborative projects, and real-world applications to make learning meaningful and engaging for all students.

Frequently Asked Questions

What curriculum does Seat of Wisdom Academy follow?

We follow the Nigerian curriculum as prescribed by the Ministry of Education, enriched with international best practices. Our curriculum is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while meeting all national educational standards.

What are the school hours?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM for all students. We also offer after-school programs and extracurricular activities until 4:30 PM for interested students.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships for exceptional students and need-based financial assistance for families who qualify. Please contact our admissions office for more information about our scholarship programs and application process.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, swimming, athletics), music and arts programs, debate club, science club, coding club, and various community service initiatives. Students are encouraged to participate in activities that align with their interests.

How do you support students with learning differences?

We have a dedicated learning support team that works with students who have learning differences. Our approach includes individualized education plans, small group instruction, specialized resources, and close collaboration with parents to ensure each child receives the support they need to succeed.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 in primary classes and 20:1 in secondary classes. This allows for personalized attention and ensures that each student receives the support they need to excel academically.

Do you offer boarding facilities?

Yes, we offer boarding facilities for students in secondary school. Our boarding houses provide a safe, supportive environment with modern amenities, supervised study sessions, and a variety of recreational activities. Boarding students benefit from a structured routine that promotes academic success and personal growth.

Seat of Wisdom Academy is committed to providing quality education that nurtures young minds and prepares them for future challenges.
