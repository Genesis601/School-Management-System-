Admission Overview

Welcome to Our Learning Community

At Seat of Wisdom Academy, we welcome students who demonstrate academic potential, good character, and a willingness to contribute positively to our school community. Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our challenging academic environment and benefit from our holistic educational approach.

We offer admission to students from diverse backgrounds, creating a vibrant learning environment that prepares students for success in a global society. Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention and foster strong student-teacher relationships.

Admission Process

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit an inquiry form or contact our admissions office. Complete the application form with all required documents, including academic records, birth certificate, and passport photographs.

2

Entrance Examination

All prospective students are required to take an entrance examination to assess their academic readiness. The exam covers English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge appropriate for the grade level.

3

Interview & Assessment

Shortlisted candidates and their parents/guardians will be invited for an interview with the admissions committee. This helps us understand the student's interests, strengths, and fit with our school values.

4

Admission Decision

Admission decisions are based on the entrance exam results, interview assessment, and review of previous academic records. Successful candidates will receive an offer letter within two weeks of the interview.

5

Registration & Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the registration process by submitting all required documents and paying the necessary fees. New students will then receive orientation materials and information about the school calendar.

School Fees

Payment Information

We are committed to making quality education accessible through flexible payment options and financial assistance programs.

  • Fees are payable per term (3 terms per academic year)
  • Discount available for full annual payment (5%)
  • Sibling discount of 10% for the second child and 15% for subsequent children
  • Payment plans available upon request
  • Scholarships available for exceptional students

For detailed fee breakdown by grade level or information about financial assistance, please contact our admissions office.
